Here's an instruction manual, based on the original instruction manual.

Origin story

Designercize is a digital version of an analog whiteboard exercise originally created by Kate Rutter and Laura Klein, and honed over thousands of practice sessions with Tradecraft designers.

The designercize app was created by Tradecraft design instructors Zac & Jake, because we <3 you. Inspired by TypeCooker. Engineering support by Patrick Burd, and prompt + emotional support by Megan Kard.

Designer, challenge thyself

"The path to mastery is paved with practice"

Do some designercize every day to achieve:

  • Better design thinking
  • Faster design decisions
  • Dates with attractive people
  • Mastery of the whiteboard
  • Dynamite interview skills
  • Happiness and success in all your endeavors

Works best with friends and a whiteboard, but screens are okay too. Challenges should be short enough to be completed over your lunch break, so you can build a habit. Here's the original instruction manual, and an updated edition for 2017.

Designercize is a digital version of an analog whiteboard exercise originally created by Kate Rutter and Laura Klein, and honed over thousands of practice sessions with Tradecraft designers.

The designercize app was created by Tradecraft design instructors Zac & Jake, because we <3 you. Inspired by TypeCooker. Engineering support by Patrick Burd, and prompt + emotional support by Megan Kard.
